WhatsUpDoc’s Documentation¶

Prologue

Here I embark on a project to document my thoughts and learning about documentation of scientific software. My goal is to collect and summarize as I learn and formulate ideas. Therefore this doc will have two sections. A working edge, where I keep my current thoughts and a more polished section, where I apply what I learned to explain my approach to documentation. Right now everything is the working edge!
