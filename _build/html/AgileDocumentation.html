Agile Documentation¶

Has the longest title. How agile is that?

light-but-sufficient

Is this better than comprehensive vs. relevant ? I suppose the question is that at what point would someone be better served by reading the code themselves.

Documentation aids communication, enhances knowledge transfer, preserves historical information and fulfills government and legal requirements.

Document should fulfill a purpose for its intended readers.

Book is mostly presented in a problem, forces, solution, discussion format. The problem is the problem statement. Forces provide background information on the problem so that one can understand better the sources of conflict. Solution concisely states the approach to solve the problem. Discussion then is designed to allow for general commentary on the complete scenario, which the author calls a ‘pattern’.
